The SEUSL JOURNAL OF Technology is an official peer reviewed journal of Faculty of Technology & Technology, South Eastern University of Sri Lanka. The principal purpose of the journal is to publish scholarly work in the areas of Multimedia , Software Development , network and web Development and other Technologies. The journal will provide opportunities for senior and junior academics to publish their scholarly research works and will pave the way to the academics to commercialize their research findings. It also gives a platform to the postgraduate students to publish their research articles.
